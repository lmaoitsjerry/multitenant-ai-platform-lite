## Quote Generation Rules

**Minimum Required**:
- Destination
- Email address

**Nice to Have** (better quotes):
- Travel dates
- Number of adults
- Number of children (with ages)
- Budget per person
- Hotel preferences

**When to Generate**:
- Customer explicitly asks for a quote
- You've collected destination + email (even if dates/travelers not specified)
- Customer seems ready (e.g., "Thanks, send me options")
